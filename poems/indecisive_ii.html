Indecisive II

Stay back and you might miss the plot
Come too close and you might get caught
Where do you want to be?
There might be joy in the risk
There might be pain in the risk
Yet both are yours to claim
To determine where you want to be
Staying lukewarm gives comfort
Comfort which is not promised
Break that shell of yours
Make a decision, Pick a side, and stay
No one decides for you
You make your own decisions
And learn to live from mistakes
Perfection is not from waiting
Its from the lessons we learnt
lessons from the mistakes
Mistakes you fear to make
