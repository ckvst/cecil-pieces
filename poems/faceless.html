Faceless

Their pace is indecisive
They pick sides for acceptance
What do you seek to be?
Who do you seek to become?
Similar statements but diffrent
You follow them to party
You attain perfect grades
You stay home to study
You attain perfect grades
In the end
What truly matters
Is what or who you become
