You don't

Leaving cracks on the wall
Cracks formed from thoughts
Either great or little
They still engraved on the wall
Water spilled on the ground
Ready to assist a slip and fall
Either little or more
the harm might be done
Pain caused by betrayal
Might be the deadliest sting
EIther from a friend or foe
Pain hits harder from close
