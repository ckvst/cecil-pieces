The Man Meant to Be

Ruthless as it may seem
It was all a scheme to undermine
You can't, foolish, bastard
All words to tear down the man
The man meant to become
True strength is in pain
Purpose from the Lord Almighty
But it was all meant to happen
To harden the man through pain
Slowly it would come to fore
That the man has truly become
What they feared he can be
What he never thought he'd be
The man meant to be
