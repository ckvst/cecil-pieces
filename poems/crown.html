Crown

Crown. Who is worthy of a crown?
A true Queen is worthy of a crown.
Crowned right from inception and been a Queen since.
Limerance? No! True love for one who is worthy of the crown.
A Queen worth my admiration, my one true Queen.


A Queen, a beautiful young lady crowned in grace.
Her crown is majestic, fitting for her and all her majesty
Her eyes are pretty, unique as her crown and lovely as her skin
Her smile is charming, her laugh is contagious
Her walk is elegant, her talk is sweet and her voice, the sweetest
Her heart is pure, her soul is golden and her mind is sharp
Her presence is magnetic, her aura is divine and her vibe is heavenly
The only worthy of my love
The only worthy of my crown
