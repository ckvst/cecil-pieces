Slumber's Malice

The night creeps into the picture
With slight warning, takes charge
Takes charge of the sky with black
Asserts dominance with another
A secondary light, moonlight
It strikes terror towards those who fear
Provides an avenue for those who "hustle"
But the night is not to blame
Misunderstood through its color
Taken advantage of for its time
You cry for the day to make hay
You complain about the night's terrors
But the night is not to blame
Slumber visits at night
So be grateful for its touch
